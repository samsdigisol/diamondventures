Water Treatment

Panel Construction

The 3D Construction System is a cost-efficient construction system that bases on industrially prefabricated 3D panels. 3D panels consist of an EPS core with a thickness ranging from 40 to 100 mm sandwiched between two plane-parallel welded wire mesh sheets (cover meshes) and inclined diagonal wires in between that go through the EPS core and that are welded to the cover mesh’s line This results in a light-weight, three-dimensional truss system with a high inherent stiffness. The clear spacing between the EPS core and the cover mesh lies between 11 and 19 mm. 3D panels are high-precision elements produced by fully-automatic Wire Mesh Welders, type 3D/48. The standard width of 3D panels is 1.20 m while the element’s length is variable (in steps of 10 cm) and depends on the corresponding field of application.



EV Scooter

An electric scooter (often referred to as an e-scooter) is a two-wheeled or three-wheeled vehicle powered by electricity. These scooters are designed for urban commuting and short-distance travel. Here are some key points about electric scooters:

  1. Electric scooters are powered by rechargeable batteries onboard.
  2. E-scooters produce zero emissions during operation, making them an eco-friendly alternative to traditional petrol-powered scooters.
  3. Many e-scooters come with features like digital displays, USB charging ports, and smart connectivity. Some models allow for removable batteries, making charging convenient.
